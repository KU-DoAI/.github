## Artificial Intelligence · Kathmandu University · MTech AI

A collaborative workspace of the MTech AI cohort at Kathmandu University. For students and researchers who want their work to outlive a semester. This organization exists to make collaboration simple, discoverable, and useful to everyone learning AI.

A place where ideas mature, research moves forward, and collaboration becomes the norm.

### Mission
Every repository here is an opportunity to learn from each other and raise the quality of our collective output.

- Provide a central archive for academic, research projects and AI engineering best practices through peer review.
- Build a growing library of reusable code, templates, and standards to easily start new projects.
- Support juniors and future batches with accessible references and examples.
